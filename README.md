## 🌀 Vision: The Electro-Active Bloom (EAB)
The **EAB** is a maintenance-free **solid-state energy converter** designed to harvest energy where it currently dissipates unused. The system is optimized for **natural and artificial gradients** (waterfalls, bypasses, industrial outflows, downpipes). Unlike classical turbines, the EAB utilizes no rotating parts, instead extracting energy simultaneously from impact, interfacial friction, and resonance.

### 📍 Use Cases:
*   **Natural Topographies:** Waterfalls, mountain streams, and steep gullies.
*   **Infrastructure Bypasses:** Dam spillways, wastewater treatment outlets, industrial cooling returns.
*   **Urban Gravity:** Downpipe systems in high-rise architecture for energy recovery.
