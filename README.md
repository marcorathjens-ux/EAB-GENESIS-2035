## 🌀 Vision: Die Elektro-aktive Blüte (EAB)
Die **EAB** ist ein wartungsfreier **Solid-State-Energiewandler**, der dort Energie schöpft, wo sie bisher ungenutzt verpufft. Das System ist für den Einsatz in **natürlichen und künstlichen Gefällen** (Wasserfälle, Bypässe, industrielle Abflüsse, Fallrohre) optimiert. Im Gegensatz zu klassischen Turbinen nutzt die EAB keine rotierenden Teile, sondern extrahiert Energie simultan aus Impuls, Grenzflächenreibung und Resonanz.

### 📍 Einsatzszenarien / Use Cases:
*   **Natural Topographies:** Waterfalls, mountain streams, and steep gullies.
*   **Infrastructure Bypasses:** Dam spillways, wastewater treatment outlets, industrial cooling returns.
*   **Urban Gravity:** Downpipe systems in high-rise architecture for energy recovery.

## 💎 Kern-Axiome (Physical Foundation)
1.  **Multi-Conversion Principle:** Synergetic combination of piezoelectric impact harvesting (central anvil) and triboelectric surface harvesting (TENG ramp).
2.  **Fibonacci Geometry:** A logarithmic spiral ramp maximizes water contact time within a compact form factor ($\varnothing$ 450mm).
3.  **Efficiency Hypothesis ($\eta$):** Calculated system efficiency of **~51.7%** (at $h=2m, \dot{m}=1l/s$).
4.  **Vector 2035+:** Implementation of MXene-doped nanobrushes and graphene-polymers for extreme durability and conductivity.

### 🛠 Technical Specification: EAB-Module "Genesis" (Type Alpha)
*   **Form Factor:** Diameter 450mm, Height 300mm. Weight ~8.5kg (Graphene-Polymer).
*   **Leveling:** Passive fluid-bearing suspension (high-viscosity silicone oil) for automatic alignment and vibration filtering.
*   **Scalability:** Modular stack-design for serial energy harvesting in cascading gradients.
*   **Durability:** Zero maintenance due to the Lotus-effect (self-cleaning) and absence of mechanical wear parts.
